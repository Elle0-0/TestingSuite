## Secure Storage: Third Iteration - Scaling and Multi-File Operations

The system is being prepared for enterprise deployment, supporting hundreds or thousands of files per user, including very large documents.

The team requires that:

- Each file remains confidential and tamper-evident
- Operations remain efficient in time and memory, even with many users and files
- Storing and retrieving files is robust and scalable

The revised solution must maintain correctness, security, and performance, and must allow smooth operations as the number of users and files increases significantly.
